Privacy Policy

How we protect your data (spoiler: we actually do)

Last updated: January 2025

Contact us: lisa@harnessthespark.com

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Service Information: Details about your career goals, business challenges, service preferences
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through third-party payment providers (we never store your card details)
  • Communications: Messages you send us, feedback, testimonials

Information Automatically Collected

  • Website Analytics: Pages visited, time spent, referring websites (via Google Analytics)
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device information, IP address
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for website functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide coaching, consulting, and support services
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries, send updates, and provide customer support
  • Payment Processing: To process payments and manage billing
  • Website Improvement: To understand how our website is used and improve it
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. How We Share Your Information

We don't sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, email services, analytics providers (all bound by confidentiality)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a business sale or transfer (rare, but legally required disclosure)
  • Consent: When you explicitly consent to sharing

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure hosting with regular security updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews and updates

5. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing (where applicable)

6. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website (via Google Analytics)
  • Improve website performance and functionality

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some website functionality may be affected.

7. Data Retention

  • Client Data: Retained for 7 years after service completion (for business and legal purposes)
  • Marketing Data: Until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Website Analytics: Aggregated data retained for business analysis

8. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (you can opt out at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
  • Stripe/PayPal: Payment processing
  • Typeform: Form submissions and bookings
  • Email Services: Secure email communication

9. International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers are based outside the UK. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers in accordance with UK GDPR requirements.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We'll notify you of any significant changes by email or through our website. The "last updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made.
